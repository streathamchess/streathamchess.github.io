15/5/14: What a season! Our club’s highly successful 2013/14 campaign is drawing to a close – these are the highlights...

After just one season in Division 2, our London League first team has secured promotion and will be back in Division 1 next season. We had two teams in Division 2, our second team having been promoted from Division 3 last year - it too had a very satisfactory season and should finish in a mid-table position.

Our third London League team is on the brink of promotion from Division 4, with two matches left to play, while our fourth team has concluded a successful inaugural season in the Minor Division with 5.5 points from a possible 9.

In the Surrey League’s knockout Lauder Trophy competition, our team is in the final against Crystal Palace, to be played next week.

We have four teams in the Croydon League, two of which have won their divisions – our C team finished top of Division 3 and we also won the Rapidplay Division.

1/4/14: A convincing win last night against Kings Head 3 keeps our London League first team at the top of the Division 2 table. We have a maximum score of 9/9 with three matches left to play, one point ahead of Cavendish 2 who also have three games in hand.

Our London League third team is also in with a chance of promotion. Gary’s team currently lies in second place behind Hackney 3.

Another team currently flying high is our Croydon League C team, in first place on 5.5/6. Next week they take on second-place CCF C in the match that may well decide which team takes the crown.

8/11/13:

Chris Baker

Yesterday we learned with great sadness that our former secretary Chris Baker has passed away at the age of 86. Chris was our club secretary from 1993 to 2002, a post he filled with admirable conscientiousness, hard work and good humour. For the last decade or so of his life he battled a series of health setbacks with typical fortitude. Although he was no longer able to play league chess himself, he kept in close touch with us - he loved to hear the latest news about our club and team performances, and every year he made a generous donation to clubs funds. Chris was very fond of our club, and we were very fond of him.

Details of his funeral service will be posted here as soon as we know them.
